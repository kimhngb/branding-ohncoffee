Our Journey & Craft

Ohn Coffee began with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between ethical sourcing and the ultimate sensory experience. Founded in 2024, our journey took us across the volcanic soils of Ethiopia and the high-altitude estates of Colombia to find beans that tell a story.

Every batch of our beans is roasted in-house using a precision-controlled process that highlights the unique terroir of each origin. We believe coffee is a two-way conversation between the producer and the drinker. Our mission is to honor that dialogue by providing an exceptional experience, from the sustainable bean to your handcrafted cup.

Our commitment extends beyond the cup. We work directly with small-scale farmers to ensure fair compensation and sustainable agricultural practices, fostering a community that thrives on quality and respect for nature.

Curated Brews & Artisanal Bites

Our menu is a living collection of seasonal flavors, meticulously tested to ensure balance and depth. We prioritize local ingredients and transparency in every recipe, offering a unique blend of traditional techniques and modern innovation.
